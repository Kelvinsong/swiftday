Freedom

Noctilucenta is a libre serif typeface. What does that mean? Noctilucenta is free for anyone to use! Not just free as in you don’t have to pay for it. (Though I do hope you see enough value in my font to.) Free as you can redistribute, use, and even modify it to your heart’s content. Noctilucenta is an open source font under the SIL Open Font License. Enjoy! You can edit this paragraph!
