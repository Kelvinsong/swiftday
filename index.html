The north american bald eagle
(Haliaeetus leucocephalus) is a bird of prey. A sea eagle, it has two known sub­species and forms a species pair with the white‐tailed eagle (Haliaeetus albicilla). Its range includes most of Ca­nada and Alaska, all of the contiguous Uni­ted States, and nor­thern Mexico.
